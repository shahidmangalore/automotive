Maruti Suzuki WagonR Summary

Variants:
The Maruti Suzuki Wagon R is offered in three variants including LXi, VXi, and ZXi.
Market introduction:
The new-gen Maruti Suzuki Wagon R was launched in India back on 23 January, 2019.
Engine and specification:
Engine options on the Maruti Suzuki Wagon R include a 1.0-litre and a 1.2-litre petrol engine. The former produces 67bhp and 90Nm of torque while the latter produces 82bhp and 113Nm of torque. Transmission options include a five-speed manual unit and an AMT unit. Customers can also opt for a CNG variant.
Dimension-wise, the Maruti Suzuki Wagon R measures 3,655mm in length, 1,620mm in width, and 1,675mm in height, while the wheelbase stands at 2,435mm.
Exterior design:
Exterior highlights of the Maruti Suzuki Wagon R include body-coloured bumpers, ORVMs, and door handles, front fog lights, electrically adjustable and foldable ORVMs, rear wiper and washer, roof-mounted antenna, and 14-inch steel wheels with full wheel covers.
Interior and features:
Inside, the Maruti Suzuki Wagon R receives features in the form of a dual-tone interior theme, a seven-inch touchscreen infotainment system with Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, and the signature SmartPlay Studio, tilt-adjustable steering, steering-mounted controls, keyless entry, all four power windows, and silver door handles. The model also gets safety features such as dual airbags, ABS with EBD, rear parking sensors, a speed alert system, a seat-belt reminder system, speed-sensing door lock, and seat-belt pre-tensioners with load limiters.
Colours:
The Maruti Suzuki Wagon R is available in six colours that include Solid White, Silky Silver, Magma Grey, Autumn Orange, Nutmeg Brown, and Poolside Blue.
Seating capacity:
The Maruti Suzuki Wagon R has a seating capacity of five people.
Rivals:
Rivals to the Maruti Suzuki Wagon R include the Hyundai Santro, Tata Tiago, Datsun Go, and the Renault Kwid.

Maruti Suzuki WagonR Price

Maruti Suzuki Wagon R price starts at ₹ 5.87 Lakh and goes upto ₹ 7.91 Lakh. The price of Petrol version for Wagon R ranges between ₹ 5.87 Lakh - ₹ 7.91 Lakh and the price of CNG version for Wagon R ranges between ₹ 7.10 Lakh - ₹ 7.17 Lakh.
